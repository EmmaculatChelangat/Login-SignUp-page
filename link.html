Group rules from the admins

We're all in this together to create a welcoming environment. Let's treat everyone with respect. Healthy debates are natural, but kindness is required and let's keep our responses as positive as we can!

Make sure everyone feels safe. Bullying of any kind isn't allowed, and degrading comments about things such as race, religion, culture, sexual orientation, gender or identity will not be tolerated.

This is so important. Being part of this group requires mutual trust. Authentic, expressive discussions make groups great, but may also be sensitive and private. What's shared in the group should stay in the group.

Give more to this group than you take. Links are welcome as long as they are relevant to the group but please don't spam.

Please be mindful of sending friend requests to other members without permission. This is a closed group and we need to respect the privacy of others. Do NOT contact other members outside the group just to sell them something.

Trying to sell to other members or recruiting them for MLM schemes will not be tolerated here.

Please be open to our doctors/counsellors/therapists etc so that you may get a better advice or just go to the hospital if you feel unfree.
